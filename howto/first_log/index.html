First time logging in

The hub has MuST and ASE installed. Each user gets their own jupyter notebook environment on the hub.

First time logging in:

  1. Click on “hub” to connect to the jupyter hub.
  2. Enter your username

  1. Choose a password (remember this password for future logins!)
  2. Click ‘Sign In’
  3. Wait for your server to start

Jupyter hub basics

Once your server is up and running, you will see your dashboard

You can open a new terminal, or create a new notebook by clicking on “New”
